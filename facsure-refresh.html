What we do

Our Services

Facsure offers a wide range of accounting services to cater to our client's specific financial needs.

Monthly management accounting services

Preparation of monthly management packs for internal decision making or for submission to financial institutions.

Taxation calculations and submissions

Our experienced tax department will prepare detailed tax calculations of all Company, Trust and Individual tax returns and ensure that they are submitted to SARS on a timely basis.

VAT calculations and submissions

We will ensure that all the VAT calculations and submissions are prepared and submitted before the monthly deadline.

Bookkeeping

Capturing of monthly bank, petty cash and credit card statements for the preparation of the monthly management accounts.

Payroll services

Weekly & monthly payslips prepared for all salary and wage earners, as well as the submission of the monthly EMP201's and Bi-annual EMP501 returns to SARS.

Secretarial services

Our secretarial department can deal with all company changes that need to be made with CIPC as well as the submission of the annual duties and XBRL.

Annual Financial Statements

Annual Financial Statement preparation in terms of the Companies Act 71 of 2008 and in accordance with the necessary Accounting Framework.

Business consultancy

Our Partners can assist with all forms of business consultancy whether it be tax structuring, cashflow projections & budgeting, business valuations or day to day decision making.
